About OneMarket

A verified signal layer for AI, markets, chips, and energy.

OneMarket is a curated, trust-weighted feed for people who actually move markets: builders, analysts, founders, and operators. Every story is sourced, scored, and ranked to reduce noise and surface what matters now.

Built to be screenshot into decks.
Transparent sourcing • Editor workflow • Growth-grade analytics

Verified inputs

We prioritize filings, earnings, primary research, and first-party announcements, then layer in reputable reporting and on-the-ground experts.

Opinionated ranking

Stories are ranked by trust score, recency, and structural impact on AI, compute, energy, and capital flows—never by outrage.

Human + machine

An ingestion worker and heuristics handle volume; editors and contributors handle nuance, context, and conflicts of interest.

For builders, by builders

OneMarket is designed as a product, not a blog. Every piece of structure—the beats, trust labels, citations, analytics, contributor workflows—is there so you can plug this into your day job with minimal friction.
