Contributor Guidelines

How to file stories that ship fast & score high.

OneMarket is built for contributors who value accuracy, speed, and clarity. Follow these guidelines to maximize your publish rate and trust score.

1. Sources

  • • Always include at least one primary or authoritative source URL.
  • • Add 1–2 secondary links for context, not for drama.
  • • Screenshots or rumors alone are not enough for Green trust.

2. Headlines & summaries

  • • Keep headlines concise, factual, non-clickbait.
  • • Use the summary to explain what happened in your own words.
  • • Add a one-line “why it matters” anchored in impact (not vibes).

3. Beats & tags

  • • Choose the most relevant beat: AI, Markets, Energy, Chips/Hardware, Policy, Other.
  • • Use tags that help search & filter (e.g. “NVIDIA”, “Data centers”, “OpenAI”).

4. Conflicts & ethics

  • • Disclose holdings, employment, or advisory roles when relevant.
  • • No paid placements disguised as news.
  • • Plagiarism or fabricated info = permanent removal.

5. Trust & promotion

  • • Consistent, well-sourced work → higher trust scores → more front-page placement.
  • • Editors may adjust trust labels; changes are logged internally.

Full details live in our internal playbook. For now, if you follow this page, you’re publishing at a professional standard.
